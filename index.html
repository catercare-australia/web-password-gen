    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I use it?

    Click on one of the numbered passphrase generator buttons above. Click again to generate a totally new passphrase.

    The + Word or + Symbol buttons will enhance the strength of the existing passphrase.

    You can copy the generated passphrase from the copyable string on the page. You should store it somewhere safe and secure, such as a password manager. You might want to write it down and refer to the written version until you can remember it. If you do so, keep it on you and do not leave it on your desk !

    Close your browser window once you're done so others can't discover your passphrase.

    Is it safe?

    The IT team at Cater Care considers the usage of that tool to be secure enough to generate any and all passphrases of varying lengths to protect all assets within Cater Care.

    Is it compliant with Cater Care IT security policies ?

    Yes. Cater Care is rolling out passphrases for all users and that tool has been chosen as the standard way to generate passphrases for the business. For more information, consult the passphrase policy document.

    Does this application send any data anywhere?

    No security sensitive information such as your selected passphrase size or the generated passphrase ever leaves your browser or is logged anywhere. Ever. Once the initial page is loaded as static files everything is done locally in your browser, nothing is sent to us.
